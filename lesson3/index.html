Site Purpose

The purpose of the Greater Dallas Chamber of Commerce’s website is to help in the advancement and protection of prosperity in the greater Dallas area. It is designed to serve as a go-to resource for all things related to business, community, and events/tourism in Dallas, Texas. Our mission is to nurture the growth and success of local businesses, enhance our resident’s quality of life, and promote our city as the premier destination for North Texas’ visitors. We do this by nurturing a pro-business climate that helps residents and welcomes visitors, and investors.
