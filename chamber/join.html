Join The Greater Dallas Chamber

Choose Your Membership

Our NP Membership caters specifically to non-profit organizations, offering a range of benefits at no cost. As an NP Member, your organization will have access to exclusive monthly training programs tailored to the non-profit sector. Enjoy discounted rates for events and networking opportunities within our supportive community of fellow non-profit organizations.

Unlock the potential of your business with our Bronze Membership package! Benefit from bi-monthly discounted rates for industry-leading training programs, focused on enhancing your business skills and strategies. Increase your visibility through our member directory and take advantage of event discounts and promotional opportunities to expand your reach!

The Silver Membership tier is designed for ambitious entrepreneurs. Gain priority access to all of our networking events, showcase your business on our homepage, and access advanced, industry-specific training modules all at a 50% discount. Enjoy all of our event discounts, exclusive advertising opportunities, and a broader promotion of your business.

The prestigious Gold Membership is tailored for established enterprises and industry leaders. This top-tier membership allows you to gain VIP access to high-profile select events to engage you with influential decision-makers. Enjoy unrestricted advertising opportunities, including prime positioning on our homepage and newsletter. As a Gold Member, you'll have access to personalized training sessions and personalized support from our chamber staff.
