Who?

The target audience ranges from local business owners to new resident entrepreneurs who are looking for resources to help them grow their businesses and become involved in the local community. These individuals are driven and have a heart for community. They seek to connect with others in their field and contribute to the city's growth. They range from average tech users to tech-savvy and are looking to stay up-to-date on the latest business and community news and trends.

Persona 1

  • Robert Duvall
  • Local business owner
  • Responsibilities revolve around managing
  • 51 yrs old, business degree, caucasian, married, father of 4
  • Goals of using this site: nourish his business, live a connected life in the community, and contribute to the city’s well-being.
  • Spends most of his time at his business. Is well connected. Average understanding of technology.
  • Uses a tablet for browsing site since he has bad eyes. Visits site in the morning and after business hours.

Persona 2

  • Mary Badham
  • New resident
  • Entrepreneur
  • 28 yrs old, Sociology degree, hispanic, married, no kids
  • Goals of using site: looking to grow her new business, wants to become a proud local, looking for networking opportunities.
  • Spends most of her time growing her business. Doesn’t know people in the area, knows a lot about technology
  • Uses her phone for browsing site. Visits site thoughout the day as she seeks entrepreneurial opportunities.

Scenario 1

Robert is looking for potential business partners and investors to help expand his business. He's looking for info on upcoming networking events and acces to a directory of potential investors and business partners.

Scenario 2

Robert believes in giving back to the community and wants to find ways to support local charities and volunteer opportunities. He’s looking for info on local community outreach programs and charity events.

Scenario 3

Mary wants to be more involved in the local community and learn more about the city's history and culture. She wants to find info on local events, businesses, and volunteer opportunities.

Scenario 4

Mary is new to the city and doesn't know many people. She needs to find networking opportunities like upcoming business events and local groups where she can connect with other entrepreneurs and successful business people.

Scenario 5

Mary and Robert need to navigate local regulations and policies that affect their business operations and interests. They need to find info on new and existing regulations and policies that might impact them. They also need guidance on how to comply with them.
