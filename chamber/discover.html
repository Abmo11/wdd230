Discover Dallas!

With a 2023 population of 1,259,404, it is the 3rd largest city in Texas and the 9th largest city in the United States

Dallas is part of the largest metropolitan area in the southern United States - It is the largest inland metropolitan area in the US without a navigatable link to the sea

Dallas is home to 22 fortune 500 companies including Exxon Mobil, AT&T, Texas Instruments, and more!

Dallas has one of the nation's largest park system: 406 parks spanning 33 sq mi, including the beloved Klyde Warren Park downtown

The Dallas Arts District is the largest urban arts district in the United States
